Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

The Closer ("we", "our", or "us") is an AI-powered sales coaching app for real estate professionals. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Voice recordings: When you use voice role-play sessions, your speech is recorded and sent to our transcription service (Deepgram) solely to convert it to text. We do not store raw audio.
  • Session data: Text-based conversation turns within training sessions are processed to generate AI coaching feedback. Sessions are held in memory and not permanently stored.
  • Device identifier: A random device ID is generated locally and used only for promo code redemption to prevent duplicate use.
  • Promo code usage: When you redeem a promo code, we record the code used and your device ID.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI role-play sessions and coaching feedback
  • To transcribe your voice input for processing
  • To validate and redeem promo codes
  • To track subscription status locally on your device

Data Sharing

We use the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Processes conversation text to generate prospect responses and coaching feedback
  • ElevenLabs: Converts prospect text responses to voice audio
  • Deepgram: Transcribes your voice recordings to text

We do not sell your data to any third parties.

Data Retention

Session conversations are not permanently stored on our servers. Voice recordings are transcribed in real time and immediately discarded. Promo code redemption records are retained to prevent abuse.

Microphone Permission

The app requests microphone access solely for voice role-play sessions. You may use the text input alternative at any time without granting microphone access.

Children's Privacy

The Closer is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
